TI Onboarding, Requirements & Payments

New to the AHCCCS Targeted Investments Program (TIP)? Start with the Onboarding Checklist below!


Onboarding Checklist

Download the TIP Onboarding Checklist and use it as a guide through key information and resources available on the AHCCCS TI website and this, the ASU TIPQIC, website.

For those with an official TI role within your organization, the Checklist will ensure you complete necessary forms and registrations.



TI Program Overview

Please see the AHCCCS TI home page for program overview and announcements box (home page).



Requirements

TI Program requirements are on the AHCCCS TI website, along with other useful information. Please find links to this information below.

  • TI Year 6 Program Requirements
  • TI Year 4, 5 & 6 Milestone Performance Measures
    • In TI Program Years 4-6, milestone incentive payments will be based on performance measures in contrast to the Core Components used in Years 2 & 3. Years 4-6 metrics align with various other applications and uses, including the CMS Core Set and Statewide TI measures.
  • Historical TI process-related milestones (Years 2 & 3) – no longer required but may related to current performance measures



Payments

  • TI Year 4, 5 & 6 Incentive Payments – please see AHCCCS TI website for payment timeline
    • Note: For Justice Y4 Incentive Payments, AHCCCS will de-weight the performance measures for determining the incentive payments for the providers. The basis for the incentive payment will be limited to the one QIC and submission of IPAT scores.
  • Use these AHCCCS TI payment methodologies to estimate your TI incentive payments

MEASURES

HEDIS 2020 (Measurement Year 2019) measure specifications are used for TI Program Year 4 and 5 performance evaluation.


TIP Measure Detail Guides

These measure-specific guides were developed to assist TI-participating Providers with understanding of the measures and TI performance measurement. They include measure definitions, why it matters, what TI measures, member population assessed, attribution methods, qualifying billing codes, and performance targets. Accommodations made to allow telehealth visits and Collaborative Care Model services to qualify are specified in the guides, where applicable.

  • Follow-up After Hospitalization for Mental Illness (FUH)
  • Diabetes Screening (SSD) / Metabolic Monitoring (APM) for People on Antipsychotic Medications
  • Child and Adolescent Well-Care Visits (W15, W34, AWC)


Health Plan Measure Guides

The following guides provide the measure definitions, denominator- and/or numerator-qualifying billing codes, and tips for the HEDIS measures. Providers do not need to be contracted with the plan to access these resources. For more information on the HEDIS measures or to get your own license, visit the NCQA website and store.

Care 1st
  • HEDIS MY 2019 Key Pediatric Measures for billing codes and tips for all of the Pediatric PCP TI Program measures.
  • HEDIS MY 2019 Key Behavioral Measures (17 Years and Younger) for billing codes and tips for all of the Pediatric BH TI Program measures.
  • HEDIS MY 2019 Key Behavioral Measures (18 Years and Older) for billing codes and tips for all of the Adult TI Program measures.

Mercy Care
  • HEDIS MY 2019 Gaps in Care Technical Specifications and PCP Billing Guide for billing codes and tips for all of the TI Program measures except Initiation of Alcohol and Other Drug Abuse or Dependence Treatment (14/34-day) measures.

United Healthcare
  • HEDIS MY 2019 Reference Guide for Adult Health for codes for all of the Adult TI Program measures.
  • HEDIS MY 2019 Reference Guide for Pediatric Health for codes for all of the Pediatric TI Program measures.


HEDIS Value Sets & Codes

Value sets and codes used in HEDIS 2020 (Measurement Year 2019) measure calculations are available at no cost. Download the 2019 Quality Rating System (QRS) HEDIS Value Set Directory from the NCQA store.



Antipsychotic Medications List

A list of the antipsychotic medications is available on the NCQA website at no cost. You will have to create an account on the NCQA website to download. To access, navigate to https://www.ncqa.org/hedis/ > “HEDIS Measures and Technical Resources” > “HEDIS Technical Resources” > “HEDIS 2020 (Measurement Year 2019)” > “Medication List Directory (NDC codes)”

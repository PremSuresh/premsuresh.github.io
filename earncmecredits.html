TI 1.0:

The Targeted Investments (TI) Quality Improvement Collaborative (QIC) will offer Continuing Education (CE) credits to all TI participants attending the Peds and Adult Virtual QIC sessions. Please note that CE will not be offered for the Justice QIC sessions; however, Justice TI participants may attend any of the Adult Virtual QIC sessions to obtain CE credit.

CE will be awarded following the last QIC session of the year. Below is a breakdown of the CE credits available for 2020, 2021 and 2022.

2022 QIC Sessions

Medical CME 36 AMA PRA Category 1 Credits™
Nursing NCPD 36 Nursing Continuing Professional Development Credits
Psychology APA 36 Psychology Continuing Education Credits
Social Work ACE 36 Social Work Approved Continuing Education Credits
Interprofessional IPCE 36 Interprofessional Continuing Education

2021 QIC Sessions

Medical CME 12 AMA PRA Category 1 Credits™
Nursing NCPD 12 Nursing Continuing Professional Development Credits
Psychology APA 12 Psychology Continuing Education Credits
Social Work ACE 12 Social Work Approved Continuing Education Credits
Interprofessional IPCE 12 Interprofessional Continuing Education

2020 QIC Sessions

Medical CME 16 AMA PRA Category 1 Credits™
Nursing CNE 16 Nursing Continuing Education Credits
Psychology APA 16 Psychology Continuing Education Credits
Social Work ACE 16 Social Work Approved Continuing Education Credits

JAICE Accreditation Statement

In support of improving patient care, Arizona State University is jointly accredited by the Accreditation Council for Continuing Medical Education (ACCME), the Accreditation Council for Pharmacy Education (ACPE), and the American Nurses Credentialing Center (ANCC), to provide continuing education for the healthcare team.

TIP Quality Improvement (QI) Workgroups

For TI Y6, in addition to the required QIC meetings TIPQIC is offering optional QI Workgroups. The QI Workgroups will be intensive work sessions, designed to assist TI providers improve their metrics related to TIP measures.

The QI Workgroups will be measure-specific and organized as follows:

  • QIW #1 - Follow-up After Hospitalization for Mental Illness (FUH) Measures: Adult PCP/BH Providers
  • QIW #2 - Follow-up After Hospitalization for Mental Illness (FUH) Measures: Pediatric BH Providers
  • QIW #3 - Child and Adolescent Well-Care Visits (W15, W34, AWC) Measures: Pediatric PCPs
  • QIW #4 - Diabetes Screening (SSD)/Metabolic Monitoring (APM) for People on Antipsychotic Medications Measures: Adult PCPs/BH Providers & Pediatric BH Providers

In addition to working toward performance gains on the TI measures, CEU units will be available to those who attend the QI Workgroup sessions. Please find details regarding CEUs here. If you are interested in attending a QI Workgroup, please navigate to the QI Workgroup of interest below and register.

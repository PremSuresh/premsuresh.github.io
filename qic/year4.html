Year 4 QIC Meeting Archive

TIP Y4: October 1st, 2019 – September 30th, 2020

Each Y4 Virtual QIC consisted of 25-35 provider organizations that participated in the AHCCCS Targeted Investments program. The Virtual QICs convened on a monthly basis for 1.5 hours to participate in peer learning opportunities and review individual and group performance in achieving the milestones.

For historical information about prior QIC meetings (e.g., Agenda, Slides, Recordings), select your QIC in the menu on the left.

For your organization's assigned Year 4 QIC meetings, see the Y4 Virtual QIC Group List.

Below is a list of the Virtual QIC sessions and their general schedule for reference. QIC sessions were held from 11:30 AM to 1:00 PM on their scheduled date.

  • Pediatric B QIC:   4th Wednesday of Month
  • Adult A QIC:   1st Thursday of Month
  • Adult B QIC:   2nd Wednesday of Month
  • Adult C QIC:   3rd Tuesday of Month
  • Justice QIC:   Refer to the Justice Y4 QIC page for dates.

Justice – Y6 QIC

For Year 6 we will be holding a kickoff QIC meeting in January 2022, on the 3rd Tuesday of the month from 11:30 AM to 1:00 PM.

Registration: Registration closed; all events have past.

Each representative must individually register for each QIC session. *Representatives will need to join via their own individual link in order to receive milestone credit. Once registered, it is highly recommended that representatives confirm the QIC meeting invites were added to their calendar (they may need to manually add registered QICs to their calendar).

In order to achieve the QIC milestone, TIP Justice participants must: (1) submit the TIP Justice Initiative Tracker by the Friday before each Justice QIC session (1/14, 5/13, and 9/16), AND (2) present updates on their TIP Justice initiatives at all 3 Justice QIC sessions.

PEDIATRIC – Y6 QIC

For Year 6, there will be four Virtual QIC Meetings, held in January, April, June and September 2022. Meetings will be on the 1st Tuesday of the month from 11:30 AM to 1:00 PM.

Registration: Click here to register for all 2022 Pediatric virtual QIC meetings.

Y6 QIC Attendance Requirement (updated February 2022):

  • To achieve the Year 6 QIC attendance milestone, sufficient representatives must attend all 4 of the QIC sessions OR 3 QIC sessions and 2 QI Workgroup sessions. Please see the Milestone documents on the Targeted Investments website for additional information. Please see list here for the number of representatives who must attend from your organization.
  • To get credit for attending the QICs, representatives will need to manually save their link to their calendars and join via their own individual registration link.

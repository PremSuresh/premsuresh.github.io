TIP Year 5 - Virtual QIC

TIP Year 5: October 1st, 2020 - September 30th, 2021

In TIP Year 5 there will be three general areas of concentration for the Virtual QICs: Peds, Adult, and Justice.

The Peds and Adult Virtual QICs will convene on a monthly basis through September 2021 for 1.5 hours to review individual and group performance in achieving the milestones and participate in peer learning opportunities. The Justice QIC will convene on a quarterly basis*.

Peds QIC Sessions include all Pediatric PCP and Pediatric BH TI participants.

Adult QIC Sessions include all Adult PCP, Adult BH, and Hospital TI participants.

For a more comprehensive list, specific Year 5 Virtual QIC sessions dates is also available: TIP Year 5 Virtual QIC Dates By Month.pdf

*For Justice dates, please refer to the Justice webpage.

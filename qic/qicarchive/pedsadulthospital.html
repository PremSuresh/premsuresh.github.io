QIC Archive - Peds, Adult & Hospital

In Years 4 & 5, the Virtual QICs convened monthly for 1.5 hours to participate in peer learning opportunities and review individual and group performance in achieving the milestones.

    Year 4 - There were six QIC groups: Peds A, Peds B, Adult A, Adult B, Adult C, and Justice. For your organization's assigned Year 4 QIC meetings, see the Y4 Virtual QIC Group List.

    Year 5 - There were three QIC groups: Peds, Adult, and Justice. Peds QIC Sessions include all Pediatric PCP and Pediatric BH TI participants. Adult QIC Sessions include all Adult PCP, Adult BH, and Hospital TI participants.

Justice Groups - See QIC Archive - Justice in menu on the left.

YOUR PERFORMANCE

The dashboards allow Providers to view their performance on their TI measures. The dashboards are now available to authorized users.


Access Your Dashboards

To access the dashboards, go to data.tipqic.org and sign in. Annual dashboards that drive incentive payment will reflect Federal Fiscal Years (October 1st – September 30th).
